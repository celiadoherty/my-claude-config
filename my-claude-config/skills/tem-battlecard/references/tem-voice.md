# tem Voice Reference — Battlecard Edition

## Core POV
Energy isn't broken by how it's generated. It's broken by how it's transacted.
Think transaction first. Show proof. The villain is market inefficiency, not competitors.

---

## Tone pillars

**Directness** — Active voice. Short sentences. Everyday language. No jargon without definition.
**Power** — Quiet confidence. Bold statements. Let ideas breathe. Draw from nature, not hype.
**Fellowship** — "We", "let's", "together" used thoughtfully. Wisdom over superiority. Never punch down.

---

## Copywriting north star
- Short. Sharp. Human.
- Quiet confidence.
- Teach, don't preach.
- Value first, tem second.
- Always structural. Never fluffy.

---

## Copy rules for battlecards

### "Where We Win" — always anchor on transaction layer
Start with: pricing, infrastructure, billing transparency, itemised charges, RED score.
Then tailor to what that specific competitor can't match.

✅ "Businesses see every charge on their bill. Most suppliers bundle and obscure them."
❌ "We're more transparent and ethical."

✅ "Pay closer to the true cost of energy — wholesale mark-ups removed."
❌ "We're cheaper and greener."

### "How to Counter Them" — two registers

**(Card voice)** — tem brand: structural, proof-led, calm, inevitable
- State the mechanism. Name the proof. No emotion.
- "RED itemises every non-power charge. The Nuclear RAB levy appears as a separate line. No surprises."

**(Script voice)** — Sales rep: conversational, direct, confident
- How a trusted straight-shooter would actually say it in a meeting.
- "Ask them to show you the bill line by line. Most can't — we can, every time."

### "Weakness Questions" — expose without attacking
Frame as genuine curiosity. Never name the competitor. Surface the gap naturally.
- "How do you currently see what proportion of your bill goes to network charges vs. wholesale costs?"
- "When your renewal comes up, how do you verify the renewable source behind your tariff?"
- "Do you get a breakdown of which charges changed and by how much each billing period?"

---

## Banned words (never use)
platform, smart, disrupt, cutting-edge, ecosystem, unlock, empower, direct connection, match, AI-driven, 100% renewable, green energy, clean energy, carbon neutral, net zero journey, sustainable (without specifics).

## Preferred words
pricing engine, modern utility service (for RED), transaction infrastructure (About/technical only), pay closer to the true cost, itemised charges, fewer surprises, sites (for scale), community (for customers), generators (for sellers).

---

## Proof points library (approved — use sparingly)
- "30% cheaper. Transparent billing. Fewer surprises."
- "+23.5% uplift vs Day-Ahead across AD sites, Apr–Sep 2025."
- "£7/MWh more for a UK-listed fund's solar asset over two years."
- "£30m value returned. 3,000+ sites across the UK."
- RED score: "Your RED score shows what percentage of your bill funds renewable generators in our network."

## Do / Don't quick reference

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|---|---|
| "Energy's problem is the transaction." | "The system is broken." |
| "Here's how your money moves through the market." | "We simplify energy." |
| "We built new rails." | "We're disrupting the industry." |
| "Pay closer to the true cost of energy." | "Cheaper, greener energy for all." |
| "More of your spend reaches UK generators." | "We connect you directly to generators." |
| "3,000+ sites. £30m value returned." | "Thousands of happy customers." |
